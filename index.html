Overview

The GenAI x Gender Tech Hackathon, organized by Amrita University's School of Social and Behavioral Sciences in collaboration with the UNESCO Chair on Gender Equality and Women's Empowerment, is a groundbreaking initiative at the intersection of AI and social impact.

In a world where AI is shaping our future, we are ensuring that future is equitable. By bringing together diverse minds, we are not just solving problems; we are redefining what's possible when technology meets social responsibility.

This hackathon brings together passionate individuals to develop innovative solutions that amplify the voices of women and underrepresented groups in technology, address gender biases in AI, and foster responsible AI practices. Together, we are harnessing the power of GenAI to create a more inclusive digital future.

FAQs

Do I need to be an expert in AI to participate?

No, but basic knowledge of AI and machine learning is beneficial. We welcome participants with diverse skills and backgrounds.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in the hackathon is free of charge.

Will travel support be provided for the onsite event?

Limited travel support may be available for teams demonstrating financial need. Please contact the organizers for more information.

What kind of projects are expected?

Projects should address gender equality issues using AI. Examples include chatbots for accessing government schemes, career planning tools for girls, or AI-powered health support systems.
