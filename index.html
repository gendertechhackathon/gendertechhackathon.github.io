Overview

The GenAI x Gender Tech Hackathon, organized by Amrita University's School of Social and Behavioral Sciences in collaboration with the UNESCO Chair on Gender Equality and Women's Empowerment, aims to harness the power of AI to address critical challenges at the intersection of gender empowerment and equality.

This hackathon brings together a diverse group of participants to develop innovative solutions that amplify the voices of women and underrepresented groups in technology, address gender biases in AI, and foster responsible AI practices.

Requirements

  • Teams of 3-5 members
  • Open to participants globally
  • Participants can be students (undergraduates, graduate and PhD students) with a valid university affiliation
  • Basic knowledge of AI/ML or social sciences or design
  • Passion for addressing issues affecting women and other underrepresented groups through technology

Timeline

Phase 1: Registration

September 5 - November 22, 2024

Register for the GenAI x Gender Tech Hackathon. Form your teams and prepare for an exciting journey of innovation and problem-solving in the field of AI and gender equality.

Phase 2: Participant Selection

November 24, 2024

The organizing committee will review all applications and select the participants for the hackathon. Successful applicants will be notified and provided with further instructions.

Phase 3: Hackathon

December 1 - December 28, 2024

The hackathon begins! Participants will work on their projects, leveraging AI to address gender equality challenges. Mentorship and support will be provided throughout this phase.

Phase 4: Finalization and Presentations

January 6 - January 18, 2025

January 6: The top 10 teams will be selected based on their project submissions.

January 18: Onsite presentations and awards ceremony at Amritapuri Campus. The selected teams will showcase their projects, and winners will be announced.

FAQs

Do I need to be an expert in AI to participate?

No, but basic knowledge of AI and machine learning is beneficial. We welcome participants with diverse skills and backgrounds.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in the hackathon is free of charge.

Will travel support be provided for the onsite event?

Limited travel support may be available for teams demonstrating financial need. Please contact the organizers for more information.

What kind of projects are expected?

Projects should address gender equality issues using AI. Examples include chatbots for accessing government schemes, career planning tools for girls, or AI-powered health support systems.
